Biography

I started AI Executives because I kept seeing the same thing. Smart executives, serious intentions, real investment. And AI programmes that produced nothing lasting. Not because AI does not work. Because the approach was wrong every time.

The standard playbook sends executives to workshops, gives them tools, and calls it a strategy. It does not change how they lead. AI only creates durable value when it becomes part of how a senior executive actually thinks and decides, in real situations, under real pressure. That is not a technology problem. It is a leadership problem. And it needs a very different kind of solution.

Before founding AI Executives, I spent 10+ years inside some of the world's most complex organisations, working with leadership teams moving from AI ambition to AI execution. I have seen every version of this play out. That experience is the foundation of what I do.

What I actually do

I work 1-on-1 with C-level executives to build AI into the daily habits and workflows that matter most at their level. Not inspiration sessions. Not overviews. Specific processes, documented workflows, and a clear action plan with real accountability. The executives I work with directly are different within weeks, not quarters.

I also speak at leadership events worldwide, running sessions that focus on what executives need to do differently starting this week, not what AI might look like in five years.

Speaking and recognition

I speak at international forums: Webit, Function1, Applied AI Nordics, AmCham Denmark, Dansk Industri. How Casper works

DIRECT. PRACTICAL. MEASURABLE.

Three principles that govern every engagement, whether a single intensive session or an ongoing advisory relationship.

01

Context before content

Every engagement starts with a deep understanding of your role, your specific decisions and your goals. There is no standard agenda. What Casper builds for you is built specifically for you. If it does not apply to your actual situation, it does not make it into the plan.

02

Outputs, not insights

The measure of a session is not how you feel walking out. It is what you walk out with. Documented processes. Automation workflows. A prioritised roadmap. A written action plan. Assets you can use and share the same week.

03

Habits, not events

One session does not change how a senior executive leads. Casper designs every engagement around the formation of daily habits and standardised routines that compound over time. The goal is not a good day out of the office. It is a permanently different way of operating.
