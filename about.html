CASPER GULDAGER

AI advisor, international speaker and educator helping executives across Scandinavia and beyond integrate AI into how they actually lead.

Full biography

I founded AI Executives out of a simple observation: most organisations approach AI backwards. They start with tools, buy subscriptions, run a few workshops, and then wonder why nothing changes. The value never materialises. The habits never form. The board keeps asking the same question.

My work starts from a different premise. AI creates business value when it becomes part of how leaders think, decide and operate — every day, in real situations, with real stakes. That is not a technology problem. It is a leadership problem. And it requires a different kind of solution.

Before founding AI Executives, I spent years working inside and alongside Scandinavia's largest and most complex organisations, helping them move from AI curiosity to AI execution. I have seen what works at scale and, more usefully, what consistently fails. That experience is the foundation of everything I teach.

What I actually do

I advise C-suite leaders on how to integrate AI into their decision-making and operational workflows. I design and deliver workshops for leadership teams that create tangible outputs — standardised processes, automation flows, use case roadmaps — not just inspiration. And through the AI Executives Course, I have systematised what I have learned into a programme any executive can work through at their own pace.

My approach is direct and pragmatic. I do not sell transformation journeys or maturity frameworks. I help leaders build specific habits, standardise specific processes, and identify specific use cases where AI creates real, measurable ROI. Then I help them make it stick.

Recognition and speaking

I have been named one of Denmark's top 10 AI influencers and featured in Berlingske's Talent 100 — a list of the most influential people shaping Danish business and society. I speak regularly at international forums including Webit, Function1, Applied AI Nordics, AmCham Denmark and events hosted by Dansk Industri.

My talks are not keynotes about where AI is going. They are working sessions about what executives need to do differently starting now. Audiences consistently describe them as the most actionable AI content they have heard — because the goal is always Monday morning, not the distant future.

How I work

I take a small number of direct engagements per quarter to ensure every client gets the attention and customisation that creates real results. If you are serious about AI adoption in your organisation, I would like to have an honest conversation about whether there is a fit. No pressure. No sales process. Just a direct discussion about where you are and where you want to get to.
