Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Controller

AI Executives is operated by Casper Guldager, based in Riga, Latvia. For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Latvian data protection legislation, Casper Guldager is the data controller for personal data collected through this website and associated services.

If you have any questions about how we handle your personal data, please contact us at: guldager@aiexecutives.dk

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact information: Your name, email address and, where provided, your phone number and company name, collected when you submit a contact form or book a call with Casper.
  • Booking data: Appointment information collected when you book a call with Casper via our calendar system.
  • Usage data: Technical data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited and time spent on the website, collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communication data: The content of emails or messages you send to us directly.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the services you have booked, including sending appointment confirmations.
  • To communicate with you in connection with your enquiry or booking.
  • To send service-related emails, such as reminders or follow-up materials.
  • To improve our website and services based on aggregated, anonymised usage data.
  • To comply with our legal obligations, including applicable tax and accounting requirements.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

The legal basis for processing your data is primarily the performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) where you have purchased or booked a service, or our legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) in improving and operating our services.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Specifically:

  • Transaction records are retained for 5 years in accordance with Latvian bookkeeping regulations.
  • Marketing and communication data is retained until you withdraw consent or request deletion.

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

5. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.
  • Right to restriction: You may request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You may request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing based on our legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at guldager@aiexecutives.dk. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Latvian Data Protection Authority (Datu valsts inspekcija) at dvi.gov.lv.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies, small text files stored on your device, to enable core functionality and improve your experience. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary cookies: Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: We do not currently use analytics tracking tools on this website.
  • Third-party cookies: Embedded content and tools (such as the booking calendar) may set their own cookies. Please refer to the relevant third-party privacy policies for details.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.
