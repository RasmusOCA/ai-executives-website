The curriculum

SIX MODULES. BUILT FOR HOW YOU ACTUALLY LEAD.

No theory for theory's sake. Every module is designed around the decisions, habits and workflows of a working executive.

Module 01

AI as a Daily Habit

Most executives use AI when they remember to, not as a core part of how they work. This module changes that. You will build a personal AI rhythm integrated into your existing calendar, meetings and decision-making moments — so that AI becomes as natural as opening your inbox. You will walk away with a habit architecture that works for your role, your schedule and your leadership style.

Module 02

Context is King

AI is only as good as the context you give it. Generic prompts produce generic output. This module teaches you how to construct prompts and provide the right organisational, strategic and situational context so that AI output is genuinely useful — specific to your business, your language and your standards. You will build a personal context library you can reuse across your most important workflows.

Module 03

Standardise Your Processes

The difference between organisations that see sustained ROI from AI and those that do not comes down to standardisation. If AI best practices live inside one person's experimentation, they will not scale. This module walks you through how to document, systematise and share AI workflows so your entire leadership team operates from the same playbook. By the end, you will have at least one standardised AI process ready to roll out.

Module 04

Automate What Slows You Down

This is where AI goes beyond answering questions and starts doing actual work. You will learn how to map your highest-friction recurring tasks — reporting, communication, analysis, synthesis — and build automations that remove the bottleneck entirely. No advanced technical skills required. You will leave this module with a working automation you have built yourself during the exercises.

Module 05

Find Your Highest-Value Use Cases

Not all AI applications are created equal. This module gives you a proven prioritisation framework to map your organisation's workflows to AI opportunity, score them by effort and return, and focus your investment where it moves the needle. You will produce a prioritised use case roadmap specific to your organisation that you can present to your board or leadership team immediately.

Module 06

Build an AI Culture

Technology adoption fails without culture change. This final module gives you the tools to identify and develop internal AI champions, build foundational AI literacy across your organisation, and create the structural buy-in that makes adoption sustainable. You will leave with a culture rollout plan tailored to your organisation's size, maturity and specific resistance points.
