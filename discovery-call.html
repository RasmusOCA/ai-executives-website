What to expect

A DIRECT CONVERSATION. NO PITCH.

This is not a sales call. It is an honest 30-minute conversation to find out whether there is a fit. Here is what we will cover.

1

Your current AI maturity

Where is your organisation today? What is already in place, what is being experimented with, and where are the biggest gaps between current state and what you want to achieve?

2

Your goals and timeline

What does success look like for you — and over what timeframe? Understanding this shapes which type of engagement, if any, makes sense for your situation right now.

3

Whether there is a fit

Casper takes a small number of direct engagements per quarter. If there is a clear fit, we will discuss what the right next step looks like. If not, Casper will say so directly — and may point you toward resources that are a better match.

What this call is not

There is no follow-up pressure, no proposal sent unsolicited, and no pushy sales process. The only outcome of this call is either a clear next step we both agree on, or a clear no — with respect for your time either way.
